DO THIS BEFORE YOU FLY

3 Things to Set Up Before You Board

01

Check if your phone supports eSIM

Settings → General → About → look for eSIM or Digital SIM. Most modern flagships since 2019 are compatible.

02

Unlock your phone

Call your home carrier and ask them to unlock it. Most do it free if you've completed your contract or paid off the device.

03

Set up an eSIM plan

Airalo is the most popular for students. Buy a 30-day plan for ~$15–25 to bridge the gap until you get a local contract.
