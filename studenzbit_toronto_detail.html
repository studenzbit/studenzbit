Housing & Neighbourhoods

The Annex

Closest to UofT St. George campus. Walkable, vibrant, and historically student-centric. Avg rent $2,000-$2,400/mo.

Scarborough

Home to UTSC campus. Most affordable area with incredible food diversity. Avg rent $1,400-$1,800/mo.

North York

York University campus nearby. Modern condos, great TTC subway access. Avg rent $1,600-$2,000/mo.
